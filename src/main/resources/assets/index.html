• List of exchanges and their companies
  • List of companies and the exchange they are traded in
  • Use the format /api/exchanges/{name}/companies to list companies in exchange with {name} name.
  • Use the format /api/exchanges/{name}/{company} to get trading info for current and yester days.
  • Use the format /api/exchanges/{name}/{company}/{day} to get trading info. {day} must be either "yesterday" or "today".
